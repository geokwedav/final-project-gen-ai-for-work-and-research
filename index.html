Introduction

This final project represents a culmination of technical skill and creative vision within the realm of computer music. Throughout this course, we have delved into the complexities of MIDI orchestration, digital signal processing, and the nuances of mixing in a virtual environment.

The objective of this portfolio is to document the iterative process of creating a professional-grade audio composition. From the initial sound design phase to the final master, each step is chronicled in the manual sections below. This site serves as a central hub for all project documentation and technical reflections.
